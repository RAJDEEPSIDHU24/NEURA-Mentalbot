About Us

Welcome to my mental health chatbot! This chatbot is designed to provide reliable information and resources to help individuals improve their mental health and wellbeing.

I understand that seeking help for mental health concerns can be challenging, and my chatbot aims to make the process as comfortable and accessible as possible.

Important: Please remember that my chatbot is not a substitute for professional help. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek help from a qualified mental health professional.

Our Mission

I believe that education and awareness are critical components of mental health. My chatbot aims to provide accurate and up-to-date information on various mental health conditions, including symptoms, causes, and effects. I also provide a variety of resources, such as mental health tests and exercises, to help you evaluate your mental health and improve your overall wellbeing.
